  1. Dango is a traditional Japanese dumpling confection made from a mixture of glutinous and non-glutinous rice flour, often served on skewers. These soft, chewy balls are a popular sweet and come in many variations, including Mitarashi Dango with sweet soy sauce, An-dango with red bean paste, and Hanami Dango in pink, white, and green for the cherry-blossom viewing season Dango is typically enjoyed as a snack or dessert, often paired with green tea
